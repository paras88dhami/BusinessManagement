# eLekha One-Page Summary (Founder and Investor View)

## What Problem We Solve
Small businesses and personal users struggle with scattered money records, unclear dues, and delayed decisions.
They often use separate tools for billing, sales, stock, and expense tracking, which creates errors and lost time.

## Our Solution
eLekha is a single mobile workflow that combines:
- Business operations: POS, Orders, Billing, Inventory, Ledger, Reports, Team roles
- Personal finance: Income/Expense tracking, Budget, EMI/Loans, Notes, Tax calculator

Result: One place to run daily operations and understand money health in real time.

## Why It Matters
- Faster day-to-day operations for owners and staff
- Better control of receivable/payable and cash flow
- Better stock visibility to avoid stock-outs and missed sales
- Better decision-making through dashboards and reports

## Core Product Loop
1. Setup: contacts, products, money accounts
2. Operate: POS/orders/billing/ledger transactions
3. Control: inventory + role permissions
4. Decide: dashboards + reports
5. Improve: reminders, trends, and recurring insights

## Key Differentiators
- Business and personal finance in the same app context
- Role-based control (view vs manage) for safer team operations
- Cross-feature connected data (actions in one module reflect in dashboard/reports)
- Mobile-first for daily operational speed

## Primary Users
- Small retail/service business owners
- Counter operators and accountants
- Individuals managing personal money, budgets, and EMI

## Revenue/Value Levers
- Higher retention from daily operational dependency
- Premium value from advanced reporting, team controls, and automation
- Better collection efficiency and working-capital visibility for business users

## What We Track (Top KPIs)
- Time to first transaction after signup
- Weekly active accounts by module (Business vs Personal)
- Number of transactions/orders per active account
- Overdue collection improvement
- Report usage per active account
- 30-day retention and feature adoption depth

## Current Priority Roadmap
- Improve onboarding and setup clarity
- Strengthen permission clarity and user trust messaging
- Improve insight readability in dashboard/reports
- Add reminders and timeline visibility for due-critical workflows

## Risks and Mitigation
- Risk: Users confuse billing, ledger, and transactions
  - Mitigation: Simple labels, in-app explainers, and guided flows
- Risk: Inconsistent UX messages reduce trust
  - Mitigation: Standard success/error and permission messaging
- Risk: Low activation due to setup friction
  - Mitigation: Checklist onboarding and strong empty-state CTAs

## Investment Narrative (Simple)
eLekha is positioned as a practical, daily-use financial operating layer for small businesses, while also serving personal finance needs.
As adoption deepens across connected workflows, switching cost rises and retention strengthens, creating a strong foundation for monetization through premium analytics, controls, and automation.
